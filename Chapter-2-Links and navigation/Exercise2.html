Plot

On the Continent, a war-torn world of elves, magic, and monsters, Geralt of Rivia — a solitary monster hunter — navigates a destiny intertwined with the sorceress Yennefer and the young princess Ciri. As kingdoms clash and secrets unfold, the trio must face their fates in a land where morality is never black and white.

Geralt of Rivia

Geralt is a stoic and skilled Witcher, trained to hunt monsters. Though often misunderstood, he possesses a deep moral code and loyalty to those he protects. With his enhanced abilities and swordsmanship, he battles creatures and human corruption alike.

Yennefer

Yennefer is a powerful sorceress with a complex past. Her ambition and strength are matched only by her vulnerability and inner turmoil. She shares a tumultuous yet passionate bond with Geralt, shaping the fate of the Continent.

Ciri

Princess Cirilla of Cintra, known as Ciri, holds a mysterious power tied to ancient prophecies. Chased by dark forces, she becomes a ward of Geralt and trains under Witchers to face the destiny that awaits her.

Jaskier

Jaskier is a bard with a sharp wit and a talent for trouble. He accompanies Geralt on many adventures, often providing comic relief and capturing their stories in ballads like "Toss a Coin to Your Witcher."
