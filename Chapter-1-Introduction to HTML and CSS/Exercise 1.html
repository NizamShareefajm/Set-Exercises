Simple Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and sautÃ© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli and carrot first, stir-frying for 2â€“3 minutes.
  4. Add bell peppers and continue cooking for another 3 minutes until veggies are crisp-tender.
  5. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil. Mix well to coat the veggies.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds. Serve with rice or noodles.
